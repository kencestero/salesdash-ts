## 1. Manufacturer Warranty Requirements

### Diamond Cargo (Douglas, GA)
**Website:** [diamondcargomfg.com](https://www.diamondcargomfg.com/warranty)

**Warranty Coverage:**
- **5-Year Limited Warranty** - Defects in materials and workmanship
- **1-Year Limited** - Electrical, plumbing, windows, doors, sealant, paint, undercoating, roof, exterior metal, fiberglass

**Key Requirements:**
- No unauthorized modifications (voids warranty)
- Shipping/freight costs are dealer/customer responsibility
- Must be factory authorized dealer to offer warranty

**Warranty Registration:** [diamondcargomfg.com/warranty-registration-1](https://www.diamondcargomfg.com/warranty-registration-1)

**To Become Authorized Dealer/Service Center:**
- Contact Diamond Cargo directly in Douglas, GA
- No public dealer application form found
- Likely requires: facility inspection, insurance, inventory commitment

---

### Quality Cargo (Nashville, GA)
**Website:** [qualitycargotrailers.com](http://qualitycargotrailers.com/warranty/)

**Warranty Coverage:**
- **1-Year Limited Warranty**

**Key Requirements:**
- **Warranty Registration MUST be mailed** (no fax/email) within 15 days
- Mail to: 7977 Hwy 129, Nashville GA 31639
- Must include: signed warranty form + copy of Certificate of Origin
- **Failure to mail = WARRANTY VOID**

**Authorized Repair Process:**
1. Dealer must verify: VIN, purchase date, original ownership
2. Obtain **authorization number** from Quality Cargo BEFORE any repair
3. Prior written authorization required for repairs at non-factory locations
4. Parts repaired elsewhere lose warranty for that specific part

**Service Center Requirements:**
- Quality Cargo does NOT cover transportation/freight costs
- Dealers cannot make additional warranties on Quality Cargo's behalf
- All trailers sold only through authorized dealers

## 3. Certification & Agreement Requirements

### Typical Dealer Requirements

Based on industry standards, to become an authorized trailer dealer/service center:

#### Business Requirements
- [ ] Valid business license/registration
- [ ] EIN (Employer Identification Number)
- [ ] Established business location (not home-based for most)
- [ ] Adequate facility for service/storage
- [ ] Proper zoning for trailer sales/service

#### Insurance Requirements
- [ ] **General Liability** - $1M minimum typical
- [ ] **Garage Keepers Liability** - Covers customer trailers in your care
- [ ] **Inventory/Lot Coverage** - Physical damage to inventory
- [ ] **Commercial Auto** - For test drives, deliveries
- [ ] **Workers Compensation** - If you have employees

#### Financial Requirements
- [ ] Minimum inventory commitment (varies by manufacturer)
- [ ] Floor plan financing or cash purchase ability
- [ ] Credit application/financial statement
- [ ] Possibly a bond or security deposit

#### Facility Requirements
- [ ] Display area for inventory
- [ ] Secure storage (fenced lot preferred)
- [ ] Service bay/workshop area
- [ ] Office space for paperwork/customer service
- [ ] Proper signage

#### Training/Certification
- [ ] Product training from manufacturer
- [ ] Service/repair certification
- [ ] Sales training
- [ ] Warranty processing procedures

## 4. Warranty Service Agreement Template

### Key Elements to Include in Service Center Agreements:

```
1. PARTIES
   - Manufacturer name and address
   - Service center name and address (Remotive Logistics)

2. AUTHORIZATION SCOPE
   - Types of repairs authorized
   - Parts covered vs excluded
   - Labor rate agreement
   - Geographic service area

3. WARRANTY CLAIM PROCESS
   - Required documentation (photos, VIN, purchase proof)
   - Authorization requirements (phone, email, portal)
   - Turnaround time expectations
   - Parts ordering procedure

4. COMPENSATION
   - Labor rate per hour
   - Parts markup allowance
   - Diagnostic time allowance
   - Travel/mobile service fees

5. PARTS HANDLING
   - Defective parts return policy
   - Core charges
   - Parts inventory requirements
   - Emergency parts availability

6. REPORTING REQUIREMENTS
   - Claim submission deadlines
   - Documentation standards
   - Monthly/quarterly reports

7. QUALITY STANDARDS
   - Repair quality expectations
   - Customer satisfaction requirements
   - Callback/redo policies

8. TERM AND TERMINATION
   - Agreement duration
   - Renewal terms
   - Termination notice requirements
   - Outstanding claim handling
```
